Instruction for everyone

  1. Your test will start as soon as you click the start button.

  2. You will be given only 15 seconds for each question

  3. Your questions will be automatically swapped once the time is up

  4. Each question will be of 1 mark and if you get 2 questions wrong, you will lose 1 mark

  5. If your marks are less than 6 then you will be considered as failed. Or 10 will be considered as excellent, 8,9 will be considered as very good and 7,6 will be considered as good.
